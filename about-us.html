ABOUT THE SCHOOL

Pioneer School, Fatehgarh Churian Road, Amritsar is managed by Pioneer Institute of Education. The institute is being managed by eminent educationist, doctors and social workers. It presents an ideal atmosphere for the pursuit of knowledge and growth of children. The Pre-Primarywing with a new building provides separate play fields for little ones.
